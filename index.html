Permissions and OAuth 2.0 Verification

DriveNest is a reliable and transparent Google Drive client. Upon your first login to the application, you may see the "Google has not verified this app" warning. This warning is due to the application requesting file editing permission and still being in the early access phase (v0.1.0). The original source code is open for anyone's inspection.

How to Login?

  1. Click on the Advanced option on the login page.
  2. Click on the Go to DriveNest link at the bottom.
  3. Start your operations securely.
